A Chance for success...some advice at the end

Summary and additional materials (books, links, articles).

As many people as many truths and experiences, and even if you do everything right, no one can guarantee you'll be a unicorn...but that doesn't mean it's not worth trying.

Finally, I'd like to remind you what to focus on when setting up a startup:

  • Conduct market research and validate your idea - Make sure you're "not reinventing the wheel."

  • Research and observe the competition - Remember, "Keep your friends close and your enemies closer."

  • Create a team of specialists with the knowledge, experience, and disposition to thrive in a startup environment - because "cooperation leads to success."

  • Monitor the market and economy, and react quickly to changes - It's better to "learn from others' mistakes than your own."

  • Consider business accelerators to support your idea - as the saying goes, "There's no shame in asking for help."

  • Secure external funding - because "you can't pour from an empty pot."

  • Be prepared to negotiate with potential investors - they know that "money makes money."

  • Remember these points, and you might increase your chances of success.
