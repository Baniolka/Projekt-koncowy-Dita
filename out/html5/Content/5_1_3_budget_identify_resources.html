Budget: identify resources

Determine what you need to create the product - capital, people, tools?

Resource selection impacts the product plan and roadmap. Aligning resources with needs is crucial for reaching your goals. A poorly defined budget can cause delays and give competitors an edge. Budget elements to include:

  • Staff costs

  • Tools and software costs

  • Material and external service costs

  • Estimated total project cost
