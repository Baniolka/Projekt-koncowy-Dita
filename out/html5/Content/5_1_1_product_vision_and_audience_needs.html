Product vision and audience needs

Identify your business objectives, form hypotheses, and understand your potential product audience's needs.

  • Objectives: Define why the product exists and what it aims to achieve (general and business goals, KPIs).

  • Business Hypotheses: Instead of guessing your vision’s success, create hypotheses. This mindset encourages experimentation and allows for roadmap changes.

  • Target Audience: Understanding audience needs shapes the product. Conduct market analysis, research potential users, and gather feedback. Include elements like:

    • Demographic characteristics

    • Needs and expectations

    • Activities and buying habits

    • Problems and pain points

    • Available technological solutions
