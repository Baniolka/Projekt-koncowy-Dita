Other business models

Short about Lean Canvas.

The canvas model isn't the only game in town. There's also the Lean Canvas, which focuses on the most critical aspects of your business and zeroes in on generating revenue and turning a profit.

Remember, a well-crafted business model can be the bedrock of your future business. But it's not set in stone. Keep revisiting and improving it as your startup (or maybe a fully-fledged company at that point) grows and evolves. That way, you'll always stay ahead of the curve.
