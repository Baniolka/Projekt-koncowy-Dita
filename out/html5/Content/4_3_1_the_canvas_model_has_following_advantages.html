The canvas model has following advantages

Advantages of the Canvas model:

  • It gives a quick and concise overview of your project.

  • It helps verify that what you're selling aligns with why, to whom, how, and for how much.

  • It lets you map out the key design elements and see how they interact with each other.

  • Allows you to understand the interaction between design elements and their ordering.
