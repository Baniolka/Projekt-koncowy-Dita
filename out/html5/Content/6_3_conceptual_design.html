Conceptual design

This is the phase where you need to focus on potential costs, revenues, and profitability.

.

Here's what to do:

  • After the market analysis, revisit your value proposition and ensure it aligns with potential customers' expectations.

  • Look for ways to add or improve features that your competitors lack or don't execute well.

  • Develop the final value proposition into a Feasibility Study for the product. Pay special attention to technical feasibility and implementation risks.

  • Create a business model that focuses on the billing model, distribution channels, and the main sources of revenue and costs.
