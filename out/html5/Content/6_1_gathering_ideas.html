Gathering ideas

This is the phase where you need to get creative and think strategically.

Here are the steps to follow:

  • Identify a value proposition for your potential audience that will capture their imagination.

  • Brainstorm and jot down all the variations of an idea that come to mind, either alone or with a team, and then choose one or two.

  • Study various information channels (customers, competitors, suppliers).

  • Apply SWOT analysis to the idea(s).

  • Conduct market research (e.g., FGI method) to understand your target group and how well your product fits with them.

  • Create a theoretical MVP to validate the concept and define its functionalities.
